FAQs about AI Art

Are my AI QR Codes copyrighted?

No. All images generated using the AI are unique and have not existed prior. They are not copyrighted and you are free to use them.

Can I use my generated AI QR for commercial purposes?

You are completely free to use images that you have generated yourself. Images generated by others must be used with prior permission.

Is my AI QR completely unique?

All images are generated based on the description and settings entered. While entering similar prompts will yield to similar images, all images are as such unique from each other, and you will not see the exact same artwork on two QR Codes. Our QR Codes don't just store simple data, they are a piece of customized brand-artwork.

How can AI QR codes enhance my marketing?

QR Codes generated with AI have customized artwork embedded into them, which adds a level of intrigue and visual appeal to them. Customers are more likely to scan your QR Codes, which increases your return on investement from marketing and improves customer experience.

What kind of information/data can I encode in these QR codes?

Our AI QR Codes can store any kind of information that an ordinary QR Code can store, from links and URLs to plain-text data. You can use these for promoting your social media, website, or add text to them as you please.

How easy is it to integrate AI QR Codes into my existing media?

AI QR Codes are just normal images that you can easily integrate into your existing media and marketing material. It is as simple as downloading the generated image and using it in your media as a regular image. We have tried our best to make the process as easy and seamless as possible for our users.

How long does it take for an AI QR Code to generate?

We have dedicated serverse running 24/7 to generate images for you as fast as possible. Normally, it will take between 10 and 30 seconds for an image to generate, depending on the resolution. This time may increase a little bit during high-traffic periods.

Why is my AI QR code not working/scanning? What can I do to resolve this?

We recommend Google Lens for scanning AI QR Codes quickly. Even so, there is a chance that a QR Code does not work because of the artwork being too strongly embedded into it. In this case we recommend that you first check if the encoded data is free of typos and re-generating the image by changing the description a little bit. Sometimes, the QR Code may not scan when zoomed in or on certain monitors. We recommend printing the QR Codes and scanning them in the desired lighting conditions to verify whether they work.

Do the AI QR Codes expire after some time?

AI QR Codes generated using QReatit never expire and will continue to work as long as you need. The only case in which our QR Codes stop is if the website they point to stop working or go off.

Is there a scan limit to AI QR Codes?

No. There is no scan limit on our AI QR Codes and anyone can scan any of our QR Codes as many times as they wish.
